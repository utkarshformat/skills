# Example phrases by category

Read this file when you need concrete calibration on what each category sounds like in conversation data. These are illustrative, not exhaustive — the exact language in your workspace will vary by vertical and customer base. The point is the *shape* of the signal, not the words.

## 1. Risk & churn drivers
- "We haven't logged in in weeks"
- "[Competitor] is launching the same thing"
- "We're moving to a single platform for everything"
- "It's just a nice-to-have right now"
- "We're re-evaluating tools at renewal"
- "Honestly, I'm not sure we're getting value"

## 2. Product & process blockers
- "It takes too many clicks"
- "The integration with [tool] isn't lining up"
- "The team doesn't trust the output"
- "Performance is too slow"
- "The customer got confused by what we sent"
- "We can't get [feature] to work the way we need"

## 3. Adoption & enablement gaps
- "My team isn't using it"
- "I just need [basic capability], not [advanced capability]"
- "We tried it but went back to the old way"
- "I didn't realize it could do that"
- "Can someone show us how to set this up?"
- "We never finished onboarding"

## 4. Relationship & stakeholder health
- "Our [champion's role] left and the new person doesn't know it"
- "The new [exec] wants to review all vendors"
- "[Champion name] isn't here anymore"
- "[Other stakeholder] is the one I'd want in this conversation now"
- "We're restructuring the team"

## 5. Growth & expansion signals
- "We're opening a second [location/region/team]"
- "Can your tool also handle [adjacent use case]?"
- "We just brought on [N] new [users/roles]"
- "We're rolling this out to [other team/department]"
- "We also use [other tool] for [adjacent thing]"
- "We just closed our [funding round]"

## 6. Commercial & viability risk
- "[Slow season] is too slow, we're auditing expenses"
- "I didn't realize we were on an auto-renewing contract"
- "We need to cut costs"
- "Can we go month-to-month?"
- "Can you send the latest invoice breakdown?"
- "We're not sure we can keep all [N] seats"
